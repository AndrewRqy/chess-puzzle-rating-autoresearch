Methodology\n\n### 4.1 Baselines\n1. **Global mean** \u2014 predict `mean(y_train)` for every test row.\n2. **Per-theme mean** \u2014 for each puzzle, average the per-theme mean\n   ratings of its intersected training themes (with a `theme_missing`\n   fallback to the global mean).\n\n### 4.2 Main model\n- `sklearn.ensemble.HistGradientBoostingRegressor` (equivalent to\n  LightGBM\u0027s algorithm, but ships with sklearn\u0027s own libgomp, avoiding\n  system dependencies).\n- Loss: `squared_error`. Motivated by the uncertainty-aware training recipe\n  from the GlickFormer paper.\n\n### 4.3 Post-hoc calibration\nThe learned model regresses tails toward the mean (a systemic property\nof squared-error tree ensembles under high label noise).
